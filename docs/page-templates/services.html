What Is AI SEO? (And Why It Matters Now)

Traditional SEO optimizes for Google's blue-link algorithm. AI SEO optimizes for large language models that generate answers — ChatGPT, Google SGE, Gemini, Perplexity, Claude. These systems don't scan your page for keywords; they extract entities, evaluate structured data, and assess whether your content provides a clear, authoritative answer to a specific question. If you're not optimized for AI search, you're invisible to a growing share of your audience — including users who never click a traditional search result. Our RankrSEO Entity Optimization Framework addresses this systematically.

How We Execute: The RankrSEO Process

Most SEO agencies follow the same generic four-step plan. We use a different framework — one built from hundreds of campaigns across SaaS, ecommerce, local service, and enterprise clients. Here's how it actually works:

  1. Forensic Technical Audit — We don't run a quick tool scan and call it done. We analyze server logs, crawl paths, render behavior, and structured data validity. This phase identifies the technical debt that's silently suppressing your rankings.
  2. Entity Map & Opportunity Analysis — Instead of a flat keyword list, we build an entity relationship map of your market. This reveals topical clusters you can own, content gaps your competitors haven't filled, and the exact queries where Google's current results are weak.
  3. Content Engineering & Authority Build — We execute in order: fix technical issues first, then build content clusters optimized for both traditional and AI search, then acquire editorial backlinks that signal topical authority to Google's algorithms.
  4. Performance Monitoring & Iteration — Every month, we measure keyword movement against organic sessions, leads, and revenue — not vanity impressions. We adjust tactics based on what's working and double down on winners.
