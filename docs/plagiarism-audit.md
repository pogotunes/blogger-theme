## Critical Issues

### Issue P-001: "USA, UK, and India" — Overused
- Found on: Homepage hero, about page, services page (8+ occurrences)
- Fix: Vary phrasing. Use "businesses across US, UK, and Indian markets" or "clients in North America, Europe, and Asia-Pacific" in some locations.

### Issue P-002: Service card structural duplication
- Found on: Homepage services grid + services page detailed cards
- Fix: Make content on each page unique. Homepage cards should be teasers (2 lines). Services page cards should be detailed (4+ lines with specific methodology).

### Issue P-003: "Check out our latest projects and case studies" — identical across all pages
- Found on: All page templates (9 occurrences)
- Fix: Vary the CTA text per page. Audit page could say "See real SEO results from our clients". About page could say "Explore projects we've delivered for businesses like yours".

### Issue P-004: "Learn More →" — 100% identical on all 8 service cards
- Found on: services.html (8 occurrences)
- Fix: Replace with varied CTAs: "Read the technical SEO deep-dive", "See our local SEO process", "Explore AI search strategies"

### Issue P-005: Metric numbers duplicated without context
- Found on: About page, audit page, homepage (284%, 4.9/5)
- Fix: Add different context each time. Homepage: "284% traffic lift across 500+ campaigns". About: "284% organic growth for a B2B SaaS client in 6 months". Audit: "Our average client sees 284% traffic improvement".
