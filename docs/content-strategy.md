## 2. Page Type Definitions

### Core Service Pages
**Purpose:** Convert visitors into leads for each specific service
**SEO Focus:** Primary + secondary commercial keywords
**Conversion:** Audit CTA, Contact CTA, Phone CTA
**Schema:** Service, FAQPage, BreadcrumbList

### GEO Location Pages
**Purpose:** Capture location-specific search traffic
**SEO Focus:** "SEO agency [city/country]" keywords
**Conversion:** Local trust signals, regional testimonials
**Schema:** LocalBusiness, Service, FAQPage

### Blog Posts
**Purpose:** Build topical authority, capture informational traffic
**SEO Focus:** Long-tail keywords, featured snippets, AEO
**Conversion:** Contextual CTAs linking to service pages

### Lead Magnets
**Purpose:** Capture emails and generate qualified leads
**Content:** Checklists, guides, audit tools
**Conversion:** Email capture → consultation funnel

## 4. Content Templates

### Service Page Template
```
H1: [Service Name] Services — [Value Proposition]
H2: What Is [Service Name]?
  Paragraph defining the service in simple terms
H2: Our [Service Name] Services
  3-4 service cards with features and benefits
H2: Why Choose RankrSEO for [Service Name]?
  4-5 unique selling points
H2: [Service Name] Process
  3-4 step process
H2: Frequently Asked Questions
  4-6 FAQ items
CTA: Free SEO Audit button
```

### GEO Page Template
```
H1: SEO Services in [City] — RankrSEO
H2: Why Your [City] Business Needs SEO
  Location-specific context and challenges
H2: Our SEO Services for [City] Businesses
  Service cards adapted for local context
H2: Why [City] Businesses Choose RankrSEO
  Local testimonials, local trust signals
H2: [City] SEO Success Stories
  Local case studies
H2: Frequently Asked Questions
  Location-specific FAQs
CTA: Free SEO Audit button
```

### Blog Post Template
```
H1: [Keyword-Rich Title]
H2: What Is [Topic]?
  Clear definition (AEO-optimized)
H2: Why [Topic] Matters for SEO
  Context and importance
H2: Key [Topic] Strategies
  5-7 actionable strategies

  Step-by-step guide

  3-5 FAQ items
```
