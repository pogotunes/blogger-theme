Our DSIOG Methodology

Every RankrSEO campaign follows the DSIOG framework — Discover, Strategize, Implement, Optimize, Grow. During Discover we audit technical health, competitor landscape, and keyword gaps. Strategize builds a prioritized roadmap tied to business goals. Implement executes technical fixes, content creation, and link building. Optimize continuously tests and refines. Grow scales winning tactics into adjacent keyword clusters. A Backlinko study found the average #1 ranking page ranks top 10 for nearly 1,000 related keywords — validating our topical authority approach over single-keyword targeting.
