What Is AI SEO?

AI SEO (also called Generative Engine Optimization or GEO) is the practice of optimizing content for AI-powered search platforms. Unlike traditional search engines that return a list of blue links, AI search engines generate direct answers by synthesizing information from multiple sources. To appear in these AI-generated answers, your content needs to be structured, authoritative, and optimized differently than for traditional Google search.

Our AI SEO Services

AI Search Content Optimization

We optimize your content to be referenced by AI models. This includes structuring content for direct answers, implementing clear entity relationships, using natural language patterns that AI models recognize, and building topical authority signals.

Entity SEO & Semantic Optimization

AI models understand content through entities (people, places, concepts, things) and their relationships. We map entity relationships in your content, implement entity markup, and build topical clusters that establish your site as an authoritative source.

Structured Data for AI

Proper schema markup is more important than ever for AI search. FAQ schema, HowTo schema, Article schema, and Organization schema help AI models understand and cite your content correctly.

Google SGE Optimization

Google's Search Generative Experience (SGE) is rolling out globally. We optimize your content for the AI-powered overviews, conversational follow-ups, and cited snippets that will dominate future search results.

Conversational Content Strategy

AI search users ask questions in natural language. We develop content strategies that target the conversational queries driving AI search, including "What is," "How to," "Why does," and comparison searches.

Frequently Asked Questions

Is AI SEO different from traditional SEO?
Yes and no. Traditional SEO fundamentals still matter, but AI SEO adds new dimensions: entity optimization, conversational content, structured data for AI context, and citation-worthy authority building.
How do I know if my content appears in AI search results?
We monitor AI search citations using specialized tools and manual testing across ChatGPT, Gemini, Perplexity, and Google SGE. We provide monthly reports on your AI search visibility.
Can you optimize existing content for AI search?
Absolutely. We audit your existing content and optimize it for AI search by improving structure, adding entity signals, implementing schema markup, and strengthening topical authority.
Is AI SEO a replacement for traditional SEO?
No. AI SEO complements traditional SEO. You still need strong technical SEO, quality backlinks, and great content. AI SEO is an additional layer that future-proofs your strategy.
