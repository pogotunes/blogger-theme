What is SEO?

SEO stands for search engine optimization — the process of using proven techniques, strategies, and tactics to make your website easier to find on search engines like Google, Yahoo, and Bing. When users search for a term related to your business, SEO helps your site appear among the top results.

Studies show that over 75% of users never scroll past the first page of search results. If your business is not on page one, you are losing potential customers to competitors who are. The only way to rank at the top of the SERP is through professional SEO services.

How Search Engines Work

Search engines follow a three-step process: crawling, indexing, and ranking.

  1. Crawling: Search bots discover your web pages by following links.
  2. Indexing: The search engine stores and organizes the content it finds.
  3. Ranking: The search engine decides which pages are most relevant for each query and displays them in order.

SEO optimizes each stage of this process to ensure your pages get crawled, indexed, and ranked ahead of competitors. For a deeper look, read our SEO FAQ guide.

On-Page SEO Activities

On-page SEO refers to optimizations you make directly on your website. These are the foundational activities every SEO agency performs:

  • Keyword research and targeting
  • Meta title and description optimization
  • Header tag structure (H1, H2, H3)
  • Content quality and relevance
  • Image optimization with alt text
  • Internal linking structure
  • Page speed optimization

Our technical SEO services ensure every on-page factor is fully optimized.

Off-Page SEO / Link Building

Off-page SEO involves activities outside your website that influence your rankings. The most important off-page factor is link building — earning backlinks from authoritative websites.

Link building signals to Google that your content is valuable and trustworthy. We use white-hat link building strategies including:

  • Guest posting on relevant sites
  • Article submissions to authority directories
  • Resource page link building
  • Broken link building
  • Web 2.0 content creation
  • Forum and community participation

Local SEO

If you serve customers in specific cities or regions, local SEO is essential. Local SEO helps your business appear in Google Maps and local pack results when users search for services near them.

Key local SEO activities include:

  • Claiming and optimizing your Google Business Profile
  • Building local citations and NAP consistency
  • Earning local reviews and ratings
  • Creating location-specific landing pages
  • Targeting geo-modified keywords
