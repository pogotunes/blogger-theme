Your Google Business Profile (GBP) is the single most important local SEO asset. It determines whether your business appears in the Local Pack, Google Maps, and local organic results. A fully optimized GBP can drive 5-10x more local leads than an unoptimized one.

GBP Setup & Verification

Start by claiming your profile at google.com/business. Verify via postcard (most common), phone, email, or instant verification. Use the exact business name that matches your physical signage — this is a critical local ranking factor. Choose your primary category carefully; it's the single most impactful field in your entire profile.

Categories & Attributes

Select the most specific primary category available. Add 5-10 relevant secondary categories. Complete every applicable attribute — service options, accessibility, amenities, payments, and health & safety. Attributes help Google match your profile to specific search queries and appear in rich filters.

Google Posts & Content

Publish Google Posts at least weekly — events, offers, new products, community involvement, or blog highlights. Posts appear prominently in your profile and create fresh signals for Google. Each post should have a clear call-to-action and high-quality image (at least 720px wide).

Reviews & Q&A

Every review deserves a thoughtful response within 24-48 hours. Positive reviews should acknowledge the customer and mention specific details. Negative reviews require a professional, solution-oriented response. Pre-populate your Q&A section with 5-10 common questions and answers to control that real estate.

Photos & Insights

Upload 30+ high-quality photos: logo, cover photo, interior, exterior, team, products/services, and menu. Businesses with 100+ photos get 520% more calls and 2,700% more direction requests. Monitor GBP Insights weekly for search queries, discovery methods, and customer actions to refine your strategy.

Frequently Asked Questions

How long does GBP optimization take to show results?
Most businesses see improved visibility in 2-4 weeks. Full optimization impact is typically visible within 6-8 weeks as Google recrawls and reassesses your profile.
Can I manage GBP for multiple locations?
Yes. Create a location group in GBP Manager. Each location needs its own unique profile, categories, posts, and local content strategy for best results.
