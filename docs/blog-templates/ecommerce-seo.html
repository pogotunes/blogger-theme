What Is Ecommerce SEO?

Ecommerce SEO is the practice of optimizing an online store to rank higher in search results for product and category keywords. It combines traditional SEO best practices with ecommerce-specific tactics like product page optimization, category structure, review markup, and marketplace integration.

Key Areas of Ecommerce SEO

✓ Product page optimization
✓ Category page structure
✓ Faceted navigation handling
✓ Product schema markup
✓ Review & rating schema
✓ Canonical URL management
✓ Thin content pagination
✓ Internal linking optimization
