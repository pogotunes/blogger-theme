Premium SEO Content

Your product pages are the most important pages on your ecommerce site. They directly generate revenue. Product page SEO ensures these pages not only rank in search results but also convert visitors into customers. A well-optimized product page is both a search engine ranking asset and a sales tool.

Title Tags & Meta Descriptions

Product title tags should follow a clear format: Product Name + Key Feature/Modifier + Brand (optional) + Category. Keep titles under 60 characters. Meta descriptions should highlight the product's unique value proposition, include a call-to-action, and naturally incorporate secondary keywords. Avoid generic descriptions — each product deserves unique, compelling copy that addresses the buyer's search intent.

Unique Product Content

Never use manufacturer-provided product descriptions. Write original content for every product. Include detailed specifications, sizing information, materials, use cases, and care instructions. Longer, detailed descriptions rank better and reduce returns. Add customer Q&A sections where shoppers can ask and answer questions — this generates fresh, unique content automatically.

Schema Markup & Image SEO

Implement Product schema markup with name, description, price, availability, SKU, brand, and aggregate rating. This enables rich snippets with star ratings, price, and availability in search results. For images, use descriptive filenames (not IMG_1234.jpg), include alt text with keywords, compress images for speed, and use multiple high-quality images including lifestyle shots and product videos.

Frequently Asked Questions

How long should product descriptions be?
300-500 words minimum for competitive products. Include key features in scannable bullet points followed by a detailed narrative description.
Do product reviews help SEO?
Yes. Product reviews add fresh, unique user-generated content to your page. They also trigger review rich snippets in search results, increasing CTR.
