What Is an SEO Audit?

An SEO audit is a systematic evaluation of your website's performance in search engines. It identifies technical issues, on-page optimization gaps, content weaknesses, backlink problems, and competitive threats. The output is a prioritized action plan that tells you exactly what to fix and in what order.

Our Audit Process

  1. Data Collection — We crawl your entire site, pull analytics data, analyze backlinks, and benchmark against competitors.
  2. Technical Analysis — We evaluate all technical factors using enterprise-grade tools and manual inspection.
  3. Content & Keyword Analysis — We assess your content quality, keyword targeting, and topical coverage.
  4. Competitor Benchmarking — We compare your SEO performance against 3-5 direct competitors.
  5. Prioritized Recommendations — You receive a clear action plan with each fix rated by impact and effort.

Types of SEO Audits We Offer
