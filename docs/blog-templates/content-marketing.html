What Is SEO Content Marketing?

SEO content marketing is the practice of creating and distributing valuable, relevant content specifically designed to attract organic search traffic. Unlike general content marketing, SEO content is built on keyword research, search intent analysis, and topical authority principles — ensuring every piece of content has a clear path to ranking.
